Carnitas

Description

Here's a recipe I learned during the pandemic! Easy to make, but takes a good amount of time to get right.

Ingredients

  • Pork butt/shoulder (bone-in skin on preferred)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Oregano (powdered is easier to use as rub)
  • Olive Oil
  • Orange Juice
  • Diced Onion

Steps

  1. Wash and dry pork. Rub salt and pepper onto pork.
  2. Combine Cumin, oregano, and olive oil together. Rub mixture onto pork. Make sure to get the edges/crevices
  3. Place in slowcooker after applying the rub. Make sure skin side is up.
  4. Add onion and orange juice to cooker. Arrangement of ingredients in the cooker does not matter.
  5. Cook for 12 hrs on low for best results
  6. After the pork is finished cooking, shred pork with a fork.
  7. (Optional) Pan fry pork, take some of the juices from the cooker and add. Will carmelize via Orange juice that was placed in the cooker before.
  8. Enjoy!
