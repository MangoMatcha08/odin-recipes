Japanese Curry

Description

Here is an image of an amazing recipe I learned from my friend's dad. He was a chef in Japan, and decided to make a new life in America. This has been passed down to me.

Ingredients

  • Curry Roux
  • Protein (Chicken is best)
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Potatos
  • Half of an apple
  • Ketchup
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep ingredients by cutting into 1/2 in squares (needs to be large enough to not dissolve, but small enough to eat)
  2. satee ingredients in the following order: Carrots, Potatos, Onions, then protein
  3. Add recommended amount of water to sateed ingredients and heat till boiling
  4. Add curry roux, make sure to dissolve the roux completely
  5. Turn down to medium heat and add diced apples
  6. Enjoy!
